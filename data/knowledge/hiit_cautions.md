# HIIT 注意事项 | hiit cautions

适用于：已有一定基础、无明显心血管风险的用户。

## 不建议直接做 HIIT 的情况

- 未通过基础健康问卷（见 healthcheck_parq.md）
- 静息胸痛、心悸、晕厥史、家族早发心脏病史未评估
- 完全零运动基础或 BMI 极高且活动受限
- 疾病急性期、发烧、感染（见 sick_day_rule.md）

## 安全使用原则

- 在 4–8 周规律中低强度训练基础上再引入 HIIT
- 每周 1–2 次，每次总时长（含间歇）不超过 20–30 分钟
- 间歇阶段 RPE 7–9；恢复阶段 RPE 3–4
- 全程必须先做 5–10 分钟规范热身（见 warmup_cooldown.md）

## 立即停止并就医（红旗）

- 胸痛/压榨感、放射至下颌/左臂
- 严重气促、说不出完整句话
- 头晕、接近晕厥
- 心跳乱跳、持续心悸不缓解

# HIIT Notes: hiit cautions

Applicable to: Users who already have a certain foundation and no obvious cardiovascular risks.

It is not recommended to directly perform HIIT

- Not passed the basic health questionnaire (see healthcheck_parq.md)
Resting chest pain, palpitations, history of syncope, and family history of early-onset heart disease were not evaluated
- Completely zero exercise foundation or extremely high BMI with limited activity
- Acute stage of disease, fever, infection (see sick_day_rule-.md)

## Safety Usage Principles

Introduce HIIT on top of the 4-8 week regular moderate to low-intensity training
- 1 to 2 times a week, with a total duration (including intervals) of no more than 20 to 30 minutes each time
- Intermittent stage RPE 7-9; Recovery stage: RPE 3-4
- A standard warm-up of 5 to 10 minutes must be done throughout the entire process (see warmup_cooldown.md)

Stop immediately and seek medical attention. (Red Flag)

- Chest pain/a feeling of compression, radiating to the lower jaw/left arm
- Severe shortness of breath, unable to speak a complete sentence
- Dizziness, near fainting
- Irregular heartbeat and persistent palpitations that do not subside
