# 主观用力程度量表 | RPE 0–10

用途：为训练计划中的「轻/中/高强度」提供统一对照。

- RPE 0：完全休息
- RPE 1–2：极轻松，散步
- RPE 3–4：轻松，可完整对话（轻度有氧、恢复日）
- RPE 5–6：有点喘，可说短句（常规训练强度）
- RPE 7–8：吃力，只能说单词（高强度间歇、有挑战的力量训练）
- RPE 9：接近极限，只能短时间维持
- RPE 10：极限，不建议日常训练频繁使用

计划生成中：
- 常规有氧推荐 RPE 3–6
- 力量训练多在 RPE 6–8
- HIIT 峰值可触及 RPE 8–9，频率有限（见 hiit_cautions.md）

# Subjective Exertion Scale: RPE 0-10

Purpose: To provide a unified control for "light/moderate/high intensity" in the training plan.

-RPE 0: Complete rest
-RPE 1-2: Extremely relaxed, just a walk
-RPE 3-4: Easy, complete conversation (Mild aerobic, recovery day)
-RPE 5-6: A bit out of breath, can say short sentences (regular training intensity)
-RPE 7-8: Struggling, can only say words (high-intensity interval, challenging strength training)
-RPE 9: Approaching the limit, can only be maintained for a short period of time
-RPE 10: Limit, not recommended for frequent use in daily training

Planning in progress
For routine aerobic exercise, RPE is recommended to be 3 to 6
Strength training is mostly conducted at RPE 6 to 8
-HIIT peaks can reach RPE 8-9 with limited frequency (see hiit_cautions.md)
