# 生病时训练原则 | sick day rule

用途：指导感冒/不适时是否继续训练。

## 一般原则（「颈部以上 vs 以下」仅作参考）

- **仅鼻塞、轻微喉咙不适，无发烧：**
  - 可以尝试低强度活动（RPE ≤ 5），时间减半
  - 不做 HIIT、大重量、一次性长距离

- **有以下任意情况 → 不训练 / 不上强度：**
  - 体温 ≥ 37.8–38℃ 或发冷、全身酸痛明显
  - 剧烈咳嗽、胸闷、呼吸困难
  - 胸痛、心悸
  - 胃肠炎：呕吐、腹泻、脱水迹象

## 恢复后重启训练

- 症状完全消失后再恢复正常量
- 第 1 周训练总量控制在平时的 50–70%，根据感受再逐步回升

# sick day rule

Usage: To guide whether to continue training when having a cold or feeling unwell.

General Principle (" Above the neck vs. below "is for reference only)

- ** Only nasal congestion and mild throat discomfort, no fever: **
You can try low-intensity activities (RPE ≤ 5), and halve the duration
- No HIIT, heavy weight, or long-distance one-time use

- ** Any of the following situations → No training/no intensity: **
- Body temperature ≥ 37.8-38℃ or chills, obvious general body aches
Severe coughing, chest tightness and breathing difficulties
- Chest pain, palpitations
Gastroenteritis: vomiting, diarrhea, signs of dehydration

Restart the training after recovery

Return to the normal dosage after all symptoms have completely disappeared
In the first week, the total training volume should be controlled at 50% to 70% of the usual level, and then gradually increase according to your feelings
